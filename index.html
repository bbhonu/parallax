A Day in the Life of the Sun

GOOD MORNING.

I rise and shine from the east,
bringing light from one part of Earth to another.

Some days I'm easy to see.

And some days I'm shy and hide behind the clouds.

Humans often think the world revolves around them.

Little do they know how exhausting it is to be the subject of 8 orbits.

I'd love to say I get to nap when the moon comes to take my place.

But I'm still up, shining my light on some other part of the world.

GOODNIGHT.
